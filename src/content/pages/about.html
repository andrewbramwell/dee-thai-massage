Our Story

About Dee Thai Therapy

Originally from Chachengsao in Thailand, Dee established her massage therapy practice in Wakefield in 2009, bringing authentic Thai therapeutic techniques to the UK. Her extensive training includes specialisations in Thai, Foot, Swedish and Oil massage methods.

For over a decade, Dee has built a reputation for excellence through her professional approach and exceptional therapeutic skills. Her practice combines traditional Thai massage techniques with modern therapeutic approaches to address various conditions effectively.

Dee's focus on personalised treatment ensures each client receives care tailored to their specific needs. Whether addressing chronic pain, sports injuries or simply providing relaxation, each session is adapted to deliver maximum benefit for her clients.

My greatest satisfaction comes from seeing clients leave feeling renewed, with reduced pain and improved mobility after their session. Dee Owner
